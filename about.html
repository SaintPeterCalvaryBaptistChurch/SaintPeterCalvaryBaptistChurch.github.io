Our Pastor

Pastor Dave Johnson

After graduating from Pillsbury Baptist Bible College in Owatonna, Minnesota, Pastor Dave continued his theological education by attending Central Baptist Theological Seminary in Minneaplolis, Minnesota, and Bob Jones University in Greenville, South Carolina, achieving Master of Divinity and Doctor of Philosophy, respectively.

Pastor Dave served earlier as pastor at several other churches across the nation before arriving at Calvary in 2008.
